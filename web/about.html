About

Tinker & Play is a collection of tutorials and experiments on reinforcement learning, with a focus on training AI agents to play games.

From classic Q-learning on Snake to Deep Q-Networks on Flappy Bird and multi-agent cooperation in Fireboy & Watergirl, we explore how machines can learn through trial and error.
